Employee Attrition Insights (Tableau)

Developed an interactive HR analytics dashboard to uncover workforce trends and performance insights, aiding data-driven decision-making.

  • Designed dynamic visualizations for employee demographics, turnover, performance, and salary equity using Tableau.
  • Utilized treemaps, scatter plots, KPI cards, and box plots to present data in an engaging and intuitive manner.
  • Implemented performance-based metrics to highlight the top-performing employees and most effective managers.
  • Added interactive filters to explore diversity, recruitment efficiency, and termination reasons by department or demographics.
  • Displayed KPIs such as total employees, attrition rate, satisfaction levels, and average performance using dynamic cards.
  • Created detailed salary analysis visualizations to uncover equity gaps across departments and demographics.
  • Analyzed recruitment source effectiveness using treemaps to support talent acquisition strategies.
  • Identified unique insights like top employee and best manager based on combined performance and retention metrics.
  • Ensured the dashboard was decision-oriented, compact, and intuitive for stakeholders across HR and leadership roles.

Tools Used: Tableau, Excel, Calculated Fields, Data Visualization, HR Analytics
